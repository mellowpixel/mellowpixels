Website for the Berlin based branch of "Allianz"

Wordpress website for the Berlin branch of international insurance company Allianz. Apart from the website development, I enjoyed working with the Allianz team on generation of ideas on how to make their busy life easier. We took their routine tasks online and added useful features like:

  • online Appointments calendar and appointments booking
  • Quote requests
  • The gallery of Insurance cases

We also had some good fun during photoshoot, when I was asked to take pictures of team and the office.
